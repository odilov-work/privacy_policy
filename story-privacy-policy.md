# Privacy Policy for Hi

**Effective Date:** 01.05.2026
**Last Updated:** 01.05.2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **IlovaUz** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the **Sahifa** mobile application on **iOS and Android**.

Short Story is a reading application that allows users to discover stories, save stories, track reading progress, leave reviews, personalize reading settings, and sign in to sync their experience across devices.

By using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

### A. Information You Provide Directly

- Your name
- Your email address
- Your phone number, if phone-based authentication is used
- Your profile photo or avatar, if you upload one
- Reviews, ratings, comments, and other content you submit in the app
- Your daily reading goal and profile preferences

### B. Authentication Information

If you sign in with Google, we may receive certain account information from Google, such as:

- Your name
- Your email address
- Your Google account identifier
- Your profile image, if available

If you use phone number authentication where available, we may collect:

- Your phone number
- OTP/session verification data needed to complete sign-in

### C. Reading and App Activity Information

To provide app features, we may collect and store information such as:

- Reading progress
- Current story part or reading position
- Saved stories
- Story likes
- Story reviews and ratings
- Reading streak information
- Daily reading completion and heartbeat activity
- Story interaction history needed to support app functionality

### D. Uploaded Content

If you upload an avatar or another image through the app, that content may be stored by our backend or cloud storage providers and linked to your account.

### E. Device and Technical Information

We or our service providers may automatically receive limited technical information such as:

- Device type
- Operating system
- App version
- IP address
- Basic log and request data
- Connectivity status used to detect whether the app is online

### F. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app may store certain information locally on your device, including:

- Authentication tokens
- Signed-in user profile snapshot
- Theme and script preferences
- Temporary reading-time data
- Cached images and content for performance

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Authenticate you and keep you signed in

- Let you upload and display your avatar
- Let you leave ratings and reviews
- Personalize the app experience
- Maintain app security and prevent misuse
- Diagnose technical issues and improve functionality
- Comply with legal obligations
